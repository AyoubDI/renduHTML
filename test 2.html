Berserk

What is Berserk by Kentaro Miura about?

Intro :

Berserk follows Guts, a mercenary with a tragic past. He joins the Band of the Hawk, led by Griffith, and they achieve great victories. However, Griffith's ambitions lead to betrayal and supernatural horrors. Guts then seeks revenge, facing monstrous foes and his own inner demons.

Characters :

(Main characters:) Guts, Griffith, Casca. (Secondary characters:) Skull knight, Zodd, Farnese, Serpico etc...

All of Berserk anime adaptations :

Berserk has certainly seen numerous adaptations over the years. Here is a chronological list of them:

  1. Berserk (1997-1998): Covers the Golden Age arc.
  2. Berserk: The Golden Age Arc I – The Egg of the King (2012): The first movie in the Golden Age trilogy.
  3. Berserk: The Golden Age Arc II – The Battle for Doldrey (2012): The second movie in the trilogy.
  4. Berserk: The Golden Age Arc III – The Advent (2013): The final movie in the trilogy.
  5. Berserk (2016): Continues the story from the Golden Age arc.
  6. Berserk (2017): The second season of the 2016 series.
  7. Berserk: The Golden Age Arc – Memorial Edition (2022): A re-edited version of the Golden Age Arc movies.
